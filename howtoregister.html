How To Register

To register a business name, business premises, and a cooperative society, you need to follow specific procedures defined by Nasarawa State MTII. Follow the following six (6) steps below to register your organisation:

1
Step
Create Account or Login

Click the "Registration" button on the Navbar, then select "Online Registration" from the dropdown menu to Create account or login. Fill out the registration form, accept the Terms and Conditions, then click "Register". Go to the email you supplied to verify your account Once in your inbox, click on "Verify email address", you will then be taken to step 2, or Login to your account if you have already registered.

2
Step
Register your Business Premises

Login with the email and the password you supplied when creating the account in step 1. Once in your Dashboard, click on "Business" the sidebar on the left side of your dashboard. Under "My Registered Business", click on "Click here to register your business" Fill in the required fields, and then click "Save".

3
Step
Generate Invoice and Make Payment

Once you clicked "Saved" in step 2, the summary of all the information entered in step 2 will be shown. Click "Pay Now", at the bottom of the page to make payment. You will be taken to Nasarawa Central Billing System to make your payment. Select your payment method, if you select "Online Payment", click "Pay".

4
Step
Online Payment

Once you click "Pay" in Step 3 above, a pop up window will be displayed with your "payment reference". Take note of your Payment reference, then click "Proceed to payment". Fill in your debit card details in the field and confirm payment. Once your payment is successful, you will be redirected to the MTII Portal. Download receipt after successful payment.

5
Step
Upload Documents

If you are registering a Co-operative Society/NGO/CBO/FBO, this step is necessary. You are to upload your by-laws and the names of ten members of the organisation at the time of registeration. If you don't have By-laws, click "Download By-laws" according to organisation type. Fill in the names of all the ten people online in case of FBOs. Save all, print out the summary for your records.

6
Step
Registration successful

Once you completed the 5 steps above, and the admin approved your registration, an email will be sent to you with your registration certificate. This means your registration was successful.

To Register Business Premises, Co-operative society, NGO/CBO, or FBO, Click the button below:
Register Business Premises/Name Register Cooperative society/NGO/FBO/CBO
